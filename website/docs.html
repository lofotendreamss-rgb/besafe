Features

Financial Overview

See your income, expenses, and balance at a glance. Add entries quickly with the action buttons.

Transactions

Record income and expenses with categories, places, dates, and notes. Filter by type and export anytime.

Financial Calculations

Select data, apply percentages or VAT, and save results for later reference.

Reports

View summaries by period (month/year). Generate and save reports.

Places

Track where your money flows — stores, banks, services. Link places to transactions for clearer records.

Categories

Organize transactions by category. Create custom categories for your workflow.
